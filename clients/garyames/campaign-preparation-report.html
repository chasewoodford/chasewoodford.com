Chapter One Campaign Preparation Report

Ready to Campaign:

  • Set up campaign headquarters. Get ready to mail, fax, phone, and meet.
  • Finalize contact info for letterhead: excite.com, efax.com
  • Read manual. Absorb, practice, and be all set to go.
  • Meet reference librarian and surf new internet sites.
  • Rank database of companies into categories by desirability. Discard few.
  • Prepare specs for next data request. Aim for better fit and focus.
  • Favorite Industries/Companies List for Networking.
  • Find organizational scheme: File:Contacts, Calendar, Notes, Reference.

Ready Sales talk:

  • Write dozens of accomplishments: “Proud of well done”. Qualities.
  • Become fluent with robust, hard-hitting proofs of skills, knowledge, expertise, abilities, strengths, traits, contributions and results.
  • Become comfortable and confident with effective capsule bio, goal statement, and phrases of full information meeting.
  • Call my answering machine, 610-668-3223. Leave bio message.
  • Have ____ minutes of polished, nuggetized, and rehearsed material.
  • Networking resources list. See Networking strategy. Gather resources.
  • Master Reference List.

Ready Writing:

  • Resume strong, accurate, complete, proofed.
  • Text Only Letter and Resume; Letter and Resume.rtf version.
  • Campaign letters good to go.
  • Final revision sent to me at garyames@comcast.net
  • Master Ad Bullet List.
