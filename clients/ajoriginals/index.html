About AJ Originals

The tradition of "Old World" wood craft furniture lives on thanks to Tony Surace who began making furniture 30 years ago combining old world techniques with state of the art tools and processes. With a team of talented craftsmen, AJ Originals continues to be an established and dependable name for high quality furniture decor and signage in the Fort Lauderdale yacht community at a very competitive pricing. There is no better way to create furniture that is a direct reflection of your unique tastes than to have it built for you. AJ Originals custom craft furniture that is perfectly suited for your yacht or home. The creation of every piece of yacht furniture or sign is carefully planned to every minute detail to make sure it reflects the customer wishes. The expertize of the craftsmen at AJ originals does not stop at yacht and furniture making, but extends to name boards and custom storage solutions. Come to AJ Originals for fantastic furniture work of incomparable quality and enduring appeal. We work closely with yacht, residential, and commercial interior designers, architects, yacht brokers, shipyards and general contractors.
