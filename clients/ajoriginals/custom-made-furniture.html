Custom Made Furniture

There's no better way to create furniture that is a direct reflection of your unique tastes than to have it custom built for you. And there are no better furniture craftsmen than our team at AJ Originals.

We build furniture that's as unique as you are; one of a kind custom yacht and home furnishings, aft-deck and cocktail tables, banquet tables and storage containers.

Whether you need a replica of an existing piece, or are looking to build a complete original, AJ Originals can custom-craft furniture that is perfectly suited for your yacht or home.

From simple and elegant teak to exotic crotch mahogany, leather coverings, corian or your preferred material, each piece is an exquisite and timeless "Old World" craftsmanship.
