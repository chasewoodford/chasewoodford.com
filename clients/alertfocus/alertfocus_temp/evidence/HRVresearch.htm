Corporate results of HRV

Biofeedback enjoyes remarkable success in transforming blue chip work places. Performance, Productivity, and Wellness results from heartrate variability training.

Evidence of biofeedback results from Shell, BP, Unilever, Cisco, Boeing, and a major electronics corp.

Pre and post training shows lasting results (within 6 weeks, and then more gains at 6 months) from heartrate variability biofeedback training. Numbers below are improvements in top 2 of 5 survey responses.

  • Wellness: (impacts absenteeism, safety, insurance costs)
    • Annoyed, angry - 65% down
    • Tense, stressed - 38% down
    • Blood pressure down 6% / 4% (as measured)

  • Performance (impacts presenteeism, individual targets)
    • Sleepy, exhausted - 40% down
    • Well-being - 43% up

  • Productivity (impacts costs, quality, yield, profitability)
    • Intend to quit - 40% down
    • Empowerment - 51% up
    • Teamwork - 50% up
    • Focused, perceptive - 30% up
