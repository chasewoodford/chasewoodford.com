Directions from Rt. 76, the Schuylkill expressway that runs from Philadelphia to Valley Forge.

  1. Take the Belmont Ave exit #338
  2. Turn away from Green Lane and the river and turn onto Belmont for .4 miles to the light at Rock Hill Road.
  3. Turn right at Rock Hill Rd - go 0.5 mi.  Park on left in front of #28. 
  4. Come up stairs shared with Camp Kweebec.

Directions from Rt. 1 City Avenue.

  1. Turn onto Bala Avenue at ~4800 W. City Avenue between St. Joseph's University and the expressway, Rt 76. 
  2. Go straight at the second light, Mongomery Avenue, the road becomes Rt. 23, Conshohocken State Road.
  3. Turn right at the 2nd light (at the Wawa) onto Rock Hill Road.  Go .1 mi.  Park in front of #28 on right. 
  4. Come up stairs shared with Camp Kweebec.
